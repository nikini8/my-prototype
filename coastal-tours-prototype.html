Maldives Island Escape

★★★★★ 4.9 · 142 reviews 7 Days Beach & Island

Escape to the crystal-clear waters of the Maldives on this 7-day island getaway. You'll stay in overwater bungalows, snorkel the coral reefs, enjoy sunset dolphin cruises and unwind at world-class beach restaurants. This package is perfect for solo travellers, couples and small groups looking for a blend of relaxation and light adventure.

✈️
Return flights included
🏨
Overwater bungalow
🤿
3 guided snorkel trips

Next departures: 15 Apr · 22 Apr · 6 May · 20 May

Arrival & Check-In

Transfer from Malé International Airport by speedboat. Welcome dinner at the resort's sunset terrace.

Reef Snorkelling

Morning guided snorkel tour of the North Reef. Afternoon free time; optional spa booking available.

Island Hopping

Full-day island hopping by traditional dhoni boat. Visit a local fishing village and a sandbank picnic spot.

Free Day

Day at leisure. Optional excursions: sunset dolphin cruise, deep-sea fishing, or paddleboarding.

Underwater Walk & Night Dive

Seabed walk with helmet dive. Evening manta ray spotting cruise.

Cultural Day

Visit Malé city highlights, Maldivian crafts market and traditional bodu beru drumming performance.

Departure

Checkout and speedboat transfer back to Malé airport. Fond farewells!

What's Included ✓

  • Return international flights
  • 7 nights overwater bungalow
  • Daily breakfast & dinner
  • Airport speedboat transfers
  • 3 x guided snorkelling trips
  • Island hopping day tour
  • Sunset dolphin cruise
  • English-speaking guide

Not Included ✗

  • Travel insurance
  • Visa fees (if applicable)
  • Personal spending & tips
  • Optional spa treatments
  • Deep-sea fishing excursion
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Laundry services
